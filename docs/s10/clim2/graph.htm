Climatographs

Climate

Two of the primary characteristics of climate are:

  • temperature
  • precipitation

A graph showing how these characteristics vary over the year is called a climatograph.

  • Temperature is plotted as a line graph.
  • Precipitation is plotted as a bar graph.
