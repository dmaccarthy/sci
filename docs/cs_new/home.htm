CS 20: Intermediate Programming

Complete these three credits first. (CSE 1120 is a prerequisite!)

  • CSE 2110: Procedural Programming 1
  • CSE 2120: Data Structures 1
  • CSE 2910: CSE Project B (Game)

For your fourth and fifth credits, complete either the Client-Side Scripting or Robotics Programming courses. Do not choose courses that you have already earned credit for!

  • CSE 1210: Client-Side Scripting 1
  • CSE 1220: Client-Side Scripting 2
  • CSE 1240 & 2240: Robotics Programming 1 & 2

CS 30: Advanced Programming

CS 30 students are expected to complete five courses at the Advanced Level. Check with your teacher to be sure you have completed all prerequisite courses before beginning any course.

  • CSE 3110: Iterative Algorithms 1
  • CSE 3120: Object-Oriented Programming 1
  • CSE 3310: Recursive Algorithms 1
  • CSE 3320: Dynamics Data Structures 1

Please see the teacher to make arrangements for completing your fifth course.
